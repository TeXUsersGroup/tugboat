Title: Controlling captions, fullpage and doublepage floats: hvfloat

Summary: extensive examples of floating layouts, complex and simple.

Full text of article: available to TUG members (join TUG); will be publicly available after the next issue is published.

Author: Herbert Voß

Publication: TUGboat volume 42, number 3 (2021), pages 267-283

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/42-3/tb132voss-hvfloat
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Graphics

Difficulty: Intermediate Plus

Abstract: The package hvfloat defines macros which place objects and captions of floats in different positions with different rotating angles for the object and caption. The object can fill a full column, a full page or full doublepage, with or without taking margins into account.
