Title: Form, pattern & texture in the typographic image

Summary: the interaction of typographic size and combination leading to form, pattern, and texture.

Full text of article: publicly available now.

Author: Charles Bigelow

Publication: TUGboat volume 42, number 3 (2021), pages 231-246

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/42-3/tb132bigelow-form
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Typography

Difficulty: Intermediate

Abstract: In this essay we examine two fundamental typographic principles, size and combination, and show that out of their interaction emerge three qualitative levels of the typographic image: form, pattern, and texture.
