Title: TEI-XML to LaTeX workflow: Issues and lessons

Summary: using XSLT, LaTeX, and Python to convert from XML to PDF.

Full text of article: publicly available now.

Author: Nicolás Vaughan

Publication: TUGboat volume 42, number 2 (2021), pages 174-179

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/42-2/tb131vaughan-tei
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Electronic Documents

Difficulty: Advanced

Abstract: In this paper I discuss some of the issues surrounding the workflow used in the production of the annotated Spanish translation of the medieval work, Salomon et Marcolfus. I explain the decisions taken regarding the XSLT transformation of the TEI-XML document, in order to produce a final print-ready PDF from the LuaLaTeX text.
