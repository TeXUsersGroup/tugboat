Title: Legal and cultural landscape of mathematics accessibility in the United States: 2024

Summary: historical and current state of legal accessibility requirements.

Full text of article: available to TUG members (join TUG); will be publicly available after the next issue is published.

Author: Jeffrey Kuan

Publication: TUGboat volume 45, number 2 (2024), pages 189–192

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/45-2/tb140kuan-accessibility
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Accessibility

Difficulty: Introductory

Abstract: In April 2024, the United States Department of Justice released new guidelines, which will mandate that all state and local institutions of higher learning comply with WCAG2.1AA standards by either April 2026 or April 2027. This survey will briefly summarize the context of these guidelines, and how they affect mathematics accessibility in the United States.

Disclaimer: this paper should not be construed as legal advice.
