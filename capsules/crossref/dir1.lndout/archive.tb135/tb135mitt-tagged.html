Title: The LaTeX Tagged PDF project—A status and progress report

Summary: review of tagged PDF tasks accomplished, in progress, and still to come.

Full text of article: available to TUG members (join TUG); will be publicly available after the next issue is published.

Authors: Frank Mittelbach, Ulrike Fischer

Publication: TUGboat volume 43, number 3 (2022), pages 268-272

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/43-3/tb135mitt-tagged
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Electronic Documents

Difficulty: Intermediate Plus

Abstract: The LaTeX Tagged PDF project was started in spring 2020 and announced to the TeX community by the LaTeX Team at the (online) 2020 TUG conference. This short report describes the progress and status of this multi-year project.
