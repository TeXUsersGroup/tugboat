Title: Metadata in journal publishing

Summary: specifying extensive metadata to maximize reuse.

Full text of article: available to TUG members (join TUG); will be publicly available after the next issue is published.

Authors: Joppe Bos, Kevin McCurley

Publication: TUGboat volume 44, number 1 (2023), pages 71-76

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/44-1/tb136bos-metadata
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Electronic Documents

Difficulty: Intermediate

Abstract: We discuss how to use LaTeX classes and BibTeX styles to curate metadata throughout the life cycle of a published journal or conference article. Our focus is on streamlining and automating much of the publishing workflow.
