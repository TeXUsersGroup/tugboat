Title: Styling R ggplot2 graphics with LaTeX

Summary: integrating R graphics with LaTeX documents in a harmonious style.

Full text of article: available to TUG members (join TUG); will be publicly available after the next issue is published.

Author: Travis Stenborg

Publication: TUGboat volume 44, number 1 (2023), pages 108-109

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/44-1/tb136stenborg-ggplot2
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Graphics

Difficulty: Intermediate Plus

Abstract: The ggplot2 package is widely used for R graphics. Example LaTeX-style rendering of such graphics is presented, achieved via annotations with embedded LaTeX markup. This allows R graphics to be integrated into LaTeX documents with a harmonious visual style.
