Title: The story of a silly package

Summary: development of the sillypage package, based on the Monty Python silly walks.

Full text of article: publicly available now.

Authors: Paulo Cereda, Phelype Oleinik

Abstract: In this article, Paulo and Phelype recollect the untold story of two friends writing a silly package just for the fun of it. The story, however, takes a turn when the TeX community decides to embrace silliness. You are invited to gather around to learn about TeX, friendship, community, silly walks, and, of course, the air speed velocity of an unladen swallow.
