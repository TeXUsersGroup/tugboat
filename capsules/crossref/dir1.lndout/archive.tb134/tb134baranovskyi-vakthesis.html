Title: LaTeX classes for doctoral theses in Ukraine: Interesting tips and painful problems

Summary: status, issues, and directions for vakthesis and mon2017dev.

Full text of article: available to TUG members (join TUG); will be publicly available after the next issue is published.

Author: Oleksandr Baranovskyi

Publication: TUGboat volume 43, number 2 (2022), pages 176-181

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/43-2/tb134baranovskyi-vakthesis
(previous doi - next doi)

Categories: Multilingual Document Processing - LaTeX

Difficulty: Intermediate Plus

Abstract: In this paper, I introduce vakthesis, a bundle of LaTeX classes for typesetting doctoral theses according to official requirements in Ukraine, discuss the current status of the project and future development plans. Some LaTeX programming tricks that I have studied are considered.
