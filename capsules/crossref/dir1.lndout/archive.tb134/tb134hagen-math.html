Title: Pushing math forward with ConTeXt lmtx

Summary: generalizing atom classes and spacing; breaking multiline formulas.

Full text of article: publicly available now.

Authors: Hans Hagen, Mikael Sundqvist

Publication: TUGboat volume 43, number 2 (2022), pages 202-206

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/43-2/tb134hagen-math
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: ConTeXt

Difficulty: Advanced

Abstract: We report on some recent work on mathematical typesetting. Our main purpose has been to make both the input and output of math cleaner and more structured. Among the many enhancements, we mention here the introduction of new atom classes that has given better control over many details. We also cover the unboxing of fenced material which, together with improved line-breaking and more flexible multiline display math, has created a coherent way to produce displayed formulas that split over lines.
