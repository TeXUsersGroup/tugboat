Title: siunitx: Launching version 3

Summary: development support from standard LaTeX tools for a major revision.

Full text of article: publicly available now.

Author: Joseph Wright

Publication: TUGboat volume 43, number 2 (2022), pages 164-165

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/43-2/tb134wright-siunitx3
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: LaTeX

Difficulty: Intermediate

Abstract: Typesetting units is an important task for scientists and engineers. When done in (La)TeX, it is natural to use a macro-based approach to carry out the formatting. I entered this area in 2008 with the LaTeX package siunitx, which I’ve looked at in TUGboat. Last year, I launched version 3 of siunitx after development over several years. Here, I will recap why this came about and how I went about launching the latest version.
