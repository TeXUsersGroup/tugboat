Title: Using spot colors in LaTeX

Summary: new support in the kernel, and via the colorspace package.

Full text of article: publicly available now.

Author: Ulrike Fischer

Publication: TUGboat volume 43, number 2 (2022), pages 167-172

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/43-2/tb134fischer-spotcolor
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: LaTeX

Difficulty: Intermediate Plus

Abstract: In this paper I recount some practical experiences with spot colors we gained while working on the third edition of The LaTeX Companion. I describe what spot colors are, how to use them for text and (TikZ) graphics, how to mix them properly, and some of the pitfalls we found and how we worked around them.
