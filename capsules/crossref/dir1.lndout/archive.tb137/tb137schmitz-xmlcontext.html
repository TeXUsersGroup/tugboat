Title: Producing different forms of output from XML via ConTeXt

Summary: making slides, handouts, etc., from a single source, using ConTeXt modes and Lua.

Full text of article: available to TUG members (join TUG); will be publicly available after the next issue is published.

Author: Thomas A. Schmitz

Publication: TUGboat volume 44, number 2 (2023), pages 186-191

Category: ConTeXt

Difficulty: Intermediate Plus

Abstract: This paper shows that ConTeXt is very well suited to produce different output formats, such as slides, handouts, lecture notes, from a single XML file. Mechanisms such as XML setups and the modes mechanism allow for conditional typesetting and filtering of content.
