Title: Curvature combs and harmonized paths in MetaPost

Summary: visualizing curvature and making curvature continuous.

Full text of article: publicly available now.

Author: Linus Romer

Publication: TUGboat volume 44, number 2 (2023), pages 236-239

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/44-2/tb137romer-curvetools
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Graphics

Difficulty: Intermediate Plus

Abstract: Most font editors offer curvature-related tools. One of these tools is the visualization of curvature via curvature combs. Another tool is so-called harmonization, which makes the curvature continuous along paths. An implementation of both tools in MetaPost will be presented. Curvature-optimized paths already play a significant role in Metafont and MetaPost and therefore example MetaPost paths will be examined for their curvature behavior.

References:
