Title: Visualizing the Mandelbrot set with MetaPost

Summary: complex numbers in MetaPost, and implementing generation of the Mandelbrot set, with optimizations.

Full text of article: available to TUG members (join TUG); will be publicly available after the next issue is published.

Author: Max Günther

Publication: TUGboat volume 45, number 1 (2024), pages 113–114

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/45-1/tb139guenther-mandelbrot
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Graphics

Difficulty: Intermediate

Abstract: With the advance of modern programming languages allowing for parallelized and optimized computation, visualizing the Mandelbrot set has become easier than ever before. MetaPost was not designed for such time-consuming tasks, nevertheless it has surprisingly acceptable performance.
