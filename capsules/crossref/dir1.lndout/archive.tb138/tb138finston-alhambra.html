Title: A pattern from the Alhambra

Summary: using 3DLDF to recreate a complex pattern, including perspective drawings and color replacement.

Full text of article: publicly available now.

Author: Laurence Finston

Publication: TUGboat volume 44, number 3 (2023), pages 378–389

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/44-3/tb138finston-alhambra
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Graphics

Difficulty: Intermediate Plus

Abstract: This article demonstrates the use of GNU 3DLDF for creating graphics based on a pattern from the Alhambra, a complex of fortifications and palaces outside Granada, Spain. It explains and demonstrates how to create a perspective drawing using 3DLDF and the technique of color replacement.
