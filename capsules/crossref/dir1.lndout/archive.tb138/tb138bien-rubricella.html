Title: Towards an inventory of old print characters: Ungler's Rubricella, a case study

Summary: character inventory of a 16th century Polish printer; using TeX and tools for linguistic research.

Full text of article: available to TUG members (join TUG); will be publicly available after the next issue is published.

Author: Janusz S. Bień

Publication: TUGboat volume 44, number 3 (2023), pages 364-375

DOI (this page): 10.47397/tb/44-3/tb138bien-rubricella
(previous doi - next doi)

Category: Humanities

Difficulty: Advanced

Abstract: The goal of the paper is twofold. One goal is to present a minor contribution to the task of describing the character inventory of a 16th century Polish printing house; for this purpose it is necessary to present also some theoretical aspects of the task. Another one is to discuss the use of XeLaTeX and other tools in the author’s workflow.
